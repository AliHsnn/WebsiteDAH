After his fourth season with the Heat in 2014, James opted out of his contract to re-sign with the Cavaliers. His midrange shots now account for just 23% of his field goal attempts, and he even gets a slightly higher percentage of looks at the rim as a 37-year-old (43%) than he did as a 27-year-old (40%).

NBA Finals Appearances
/// NBA Finals Appearances ///

LeBron has been to 8 straight NBA Finals (2011-2018). He made the first four trips with the Miami Heat (2011-2014) and the last four with the Cleveland Cavaliers (2015-2018).
